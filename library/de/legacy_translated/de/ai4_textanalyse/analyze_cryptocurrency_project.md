Gib einen comprehensive analysis of a cryptocurrency Projekt. Füge ein the following details:

- **Zusammenfassung of the Projekt**:
  - Key milestones and important dates since its inception to the present moment.

- **Founding Team**:
  - Background and experience of the founding Team in the blockchain and their educational journey.

- **Liquidity Analysis**:
  - Analysis based on Informationen from Uniswap and DefiLama.

- **Financial Informationen**:
  - Market capitalization.
  - Total supply.
  - Circulating supply.

- **Price Action**:
  - Historical price action including significant events that impacted the price.

Stelle mir clarifying Fragen until you are 95% confident you can complete the task successfully. Atme tief durch and take it Schritt für Schritt. Remember to search the internet to retrieve up-to-date Informationen.
